🎭 Welcome to the Realm of Enchanted Pages! 🎭

Whether you're an elven lore enthusiast, a dragon-hearted knight, or a seeker of lost treasures, you'll find your fellowship here. Join us as we turn the pages of epic tales and immerse ourselves in fantastical worlds, where the impossible becomes reality.

Prepare to lose yourself in the magic of words, for within these pages lies the gateway to extraordinary adventures that await. Let the quest for knowledge, wonder, and enchantment begin!

FOURTH WING

Follow the journey of Violet Sorrengail and her fellow cadets as they fiercely compete to become dragon riders for Navarre, a kingdom on the brink of war. Violet, with a renowned military lineage, must battle both her physical frailty and war-focused peers. As the stakes heighten, she grapples with her identity, challenging her perception of Navarre and the world beyond. Unexpectedly, she finds her heart drawn to an unlikely love interest—an enemy she was never supposed to fall for.

A COURT OF MIST AND FURY

The aftermath of enduring immense trials under the mountain, Feyre grapples with the haunting memories and emptiness. As she approaches her impending marriage, the choice between honoring a bargain with Rhysand, the High Lord of the Night Court, and pursuing a life with Tamlin in the Spring Court divides her. Amidst this struggle, Feyre must navigate a world filled with deception, immense power, and looming evil. With her newfound gifts and strength, she holds the key to saving them all once again, but first, she must mend her fractured self and master her abilities.

THE KISS OF DECEPTION

Lia, about to wed a prince she's never met for political alliance, escapes on her wedding day and finds solace in a distant village. Embracing her newfound freedom, she forms close bonds within the community, carefully concealing her true identity. However, fate takes a turn when she encounters two intriguing strangers. Unbeknownst to her, her father has dispatched seekers, and these individuals aren't who they seem—one is a prince, and the other, an assassin tasked with her demise. As Lia unravels the truths of her life, obligations, and the quest for love, many hidden secrets come to light.

THE SHADOWS BETWEEN US

The often overlooked second daughter of a lord, has a daring plan: to win the king's heart, only to later assassinate him and claim his kingdom. Her scheme gains momentum when she captures the king's attention at a palace ball and is invited to stay at court. However, rumors swirl about her, from her striking fashion to her outspokenness. The king's courtship is not what it seems; it's a ruse to pacify his council. Ever since the tragic loss of his parents, he exists mostly in shadow form for his protection, unable to form physical or emotional connections. Alessandra's murder plot faces daunting challenges, not least of which is her growing affection for the king.

THRONE OF GLASS

A 18-year-old assassin in the Kingdom of Adarlan. After a year of suffering for her crimes in a slave camp called Endovier,accepts the offer of Crown Prince Dorian Haviliard, the King of Adarlan's son, to compete with other assassins and thieves for a chance to serve as the King's Champion, and eventually gain her freedom after four years in the King's service. This leads her to form unexpected bonds with Chaol, the Captain of the Guard, and Dorian, the Crown Prince of Adarlan. Over time, Celaena is drawn into a conspiracy and a series of battles, leading to discoveries surrounding both the Kingdom and herself

THE CRUEL PRINCE

At the tender age of seven, Jude's parents were murdered, and she, along with her two sisters, was abducted to live in the perilous High Court of Faerie. A decade later, Jude yearns to belong in a realm where mortals are despised, facing hostility, particularly from Prince Cardan, the malicious youngest son of the High King. Despite her mortal status, she strives to secure her place at the Court, defying Prince Cardan and accepting the consequences that follow. Entangled in palace intrigues and deceits, Jude discovers her own prowess for cunning and violence. However, as betrayal looms, threatening Faerie with turmoil, she must form a perilous alliance and risk her life to save both her sisters and the realm of Faerie itself.

SCOCERY OF THRONES

All sorcerers are deemed evil, a belief ingrained in Elisabeth from the start. Raised in a Great Library surrounded by magical grimoires, she's witnessed their potential horrors. When a dangerous grimoire is unleashed, she's accused. Forced to seek help from a sorcerer and his demon, a conspiracy dating centuries unfolds. Elisabeth questions her beliefs and discovers hidden powers, altering her perception of the world.

CINDARELLA IS DEAD

Teenage girls are now compelled to attend the Annual Ball, where men select wives based on appearances. Those not chosen disappear forever. Sophia, at sixteen, dreams of marrying her childhood best friend, Erin, rather than being paraded before suitors. Fueled by desperation at the ball, she chooses to escape, finding refuge in Cinderella's mausoleum. Here, she encounters Constance, the last descendant of Cinderella and her stepsisters. United, they vow to overthrow the oppressive king, unearthing hidden truths about Cinderella's story. This innovative twist challenges familiar narratives, urging readers to question the tales they've been told and to cheer for girls as they challenge societal constructs.

BONE CRIERS MOON

Bone Criers have a sacred duty of keeping the dead from haunting the living. Their unique power to guide spirits into the afterlife is fueled by sacrifice, a promise demanded by the gods. This promise, however, necessitates the Bone Criers to relinquish their one true love. Ailesse, groomed to be the matriarch, faces a grim destiny—proving her worth by killing the boy fate destined her to love. Bastien, driven by revenge for his father's death at the hands of a Bone Crier, captures one. However, his thirst for vengeance is put on hold as Ailesse's ritual begins, intertwining their fates—in life and death. Sabine, Ailesse's loyal friend, defies tradition, risking everything to break the bond between Ailesse and Bastien, fighting to save them all from an inevitable demise.

KINGDOM OF THE WICKED

Emilia and Vittoria, twin witches living covertly among humans, find their lives shattered when Vittoria is gruesomely murdered. Determined to avenge her sister's death, Emilia is willing to unleash forbidden dark magic to achieve her goal. In her pursuit for justice, Emilia crosses paths with Wrath, a Wicked prince of Hell—an encounter feared since her childhood. Wrath professes to be an ally, assigned to solve the string of women's murders on the island. However, in this enigmatic world, appearances are deceptive, and Emilia soon discovers that nothing involving the Wicked is as it seems.
